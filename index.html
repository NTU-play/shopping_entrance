Instructions to buy an item:

  1. Browse through the item list.
  2. Select the item and negotiate with the seller.
  3. Once the deal has been made, indicate whether to use our $1-delivery service(limited to NTU area only) or collect by yourself.
  4. Make payment to NTUPLAY.(Extra $1 if you are using our delivery service)
  5. We will get your item delivered in time.

Normal FAQ:

  • How should I pay?

    You can make a payment to us after you have comfirmed the deal with the seller. You can pay by PAYNOW or PAYLAH.

  • Do you charge a fee on delivery?

    Yes, we provide quality delivery service with only $1.

  • Do you charge any fee?

    We do not charge any fee on using our platform. We only charge $1 on using our delivery service. The delivery service is only limited in NTU.

  • How long it takes to receive my item?

    You can decide on when to receive your goods, but it depends on the availability of our team members.

  • Can I refund the item?

    We are not responsible for any damage caused by the sellers or buyers. However, you can contact the seller directly.

  • Where can I receive my item?

    Anywhere in the NTU campus.

  • How much is the transaction fee?

    We do not charge any fee on transaction. We only apply a $1 charges on the delivery.
